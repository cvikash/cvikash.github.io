    <div class="content-card">
      <h2>Current Work</h2>
      <p>
        My current research focuses on understanding the complex neural dynamics that govern behavioral 
        states in zebrafish. Through advanced imaging techniques including two-photon calcium imaging 
        and optogenetics, combined with computational modeling and machine learning approaches, I aim to 
        uncover the fundamental principles that regulate internal state transitions.
      </p>
      <p>
        This work has implications for understanding how the brain maintains homeostasis, how states 
        are encoded in neural circuits, and how disruptions in state regulation might contribute to 
        various neurological conditions.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="content-card">
      <h2>Methodology</h2>
      <p>My research employs a multidisciplinary approach combining:</p>
      <ul class="methodology-list">
        <li><strong>Advanced live brain Imaging:</strong> Single and two-photon calcium imaging, tracking sysytem, light-sheet microscopy, and functional imaging</li>
        <li><strong>Behavioral Analysis:</strong> Automated tracking, state classification, and behavioral phenotyping</li>
        <li><strong>Computational Methods:</strong> Machine learning, deep learning, network analysis, and dynamical systems modeling</li>
        <li><strong>Optogenetics:</strong> Targeted manipulation of neural circuits to understand causality</li>
